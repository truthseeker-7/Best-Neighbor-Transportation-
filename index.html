Best Neighbor Transportation Where every ride is a step closer to health, served with a side of convenience. We’re not just a service; we’re your healthcare journey’s best friend

MEDICAL TRANSPORTATION SERVICES

  • Ambulatory and Wheelchair Transportation: Specialized, safe transport for individuals regardless of mobility challenges.
  • Timely Appointments and Care Access: Ensuring punctual arrival for medical appointments, therapies, and treatments.

VEHICLE FLEET AND SAFETY

  • Modern, Accessible Vehicles: A fleet equipped to accommodate various needs, ensuring comfort and safety.
  • Comprehensive Safety Measures: Adherence to stringent safety protocols for peace of mind during every journey.

COMMUNITY HEALTH INITIATIVES

  • Healthcare Access Support: Collaborations with healthcare providers to improve community health access.
  • Education and Awareness Campaigns: Promoting health literacy and preventive care awareness among clients and the community.

SUPPORT BEYOND TRANSPORT

  • Partnerships for Broader Access: Connecting clients with essential non-medical community services and resources.
  • Empowerment Through Mobility: Enhancing the independence of clients by providing reliable transport, fostering self-reliance and access to life-enriching opportunities.

Best Neighbor Transportation offers reliable, compassionate NEMT services for both ambulatory and wheelchair clients. Our commitment to punctuality and safety has built us a stellar track record. We ensure every journey to healthcare appointments is accessible and stress-free. With us, you’re more than a passenger—you’re a valued member of our community.

OUR CORPORATE PURPOSE

  • Enhancing Access: Connecting individuals with medical services through reliable transportation.
  • Promoting Health Equity: Overcoming barriers to healthcare with tailored transportation solutions.
  • Empowering Patients: Offering safe, efficient rides to improve health and well-being.
  • Bridging Gaps: Linking the community to essential healthcare and wellness programs.
